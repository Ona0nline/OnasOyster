Garage Rooftop

Feeling:Cloud watching in a lavender field

I initially discovered this masterpiece, in 2021, when I felt like I needed new music but didnt know what genre I was looking for. I ended up searching "alt rnb" on youtube and it was the first video that popped up. It heavily influenced what my taste is today. What I especially love about this video is the beautiful depiction of what love looks/feels like. The production quality is also incredible.
